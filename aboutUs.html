History Of TSS

The Technical Students Society (T.S.S.) is a student body of the CSE Department, GNDU, Asr. It is established in the year 2003 as an answer to the student's dire need for a larger platform where they could help each other in building up a better, healthier student community life. After dissolving in 2006, the TSS, like a phoenix, was reborn in March 2008 from the ashes of its past. Since then it has been successfully organizing various Technical and Cultural events.

Aim Of TSS

  • - To foster & develop all students activities.
  • - To promote & develop organizational abilities in student.
  • - To develop a well-informed, articulate & participative student community life & to increase social awareness.
